getContextPath
如果地址是http://127.0.0.1:8080/ssh_0902/index.jsp则返回/ssh_0902
getQueryString
如果是http://127.0.0.1:8080/ssh_0902/index.jsp?sdf=sdf则返回sdf=sdf
getRequestedSessionId
获得SessionId

getRequestURI()
返回/ssh_0902/index.jsp
getRequestURL()
返回http://127.0.0.1:8080/ssh_0902/index.jsp
都不会带有QueryString

getServletPath
返回/index.jsp
